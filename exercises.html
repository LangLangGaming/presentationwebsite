EXERCISE ONE

Fill in the blanks with Past Simple or Past Continuous

1. We ________ to the cinema yesterday. (to go)

2. I_________ my homework for the whole evening yesterday. (to do)

3. Zhi Xun ___________ the piano at 4pm yesterday. (to play)

4. When Leshon came home, his brother _________ TV. (to watch)

5. They _________ a new house last month.(to buy)

6. We_________ our relatives in Cambodia two years ago. (to visit)

7. I ________ for the test from 10am till 5pm yesterday. (to prepare)

8. Shan Yu _______ Jerome last week. (to see)

9. What _______ he _______ the whole evening yesterday? (to fix)

10. At half past six yesterday, we ______ dinner (to have)
